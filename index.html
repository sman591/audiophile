Audiophiles

Every day, millions of people listen to music across the globe. However, not all music quality is shared alike. Certain people in the world, "Audiophiles" as they call themselves, strive for the upmost quality in the music they listen to.

Scroll down to learn more about the people that love sound.

Factors

Various factors go into the audiophile standard of quality.

Source Learn More

There are many different sources of music out there, from internet radio, to iPod, to the vintage LP, each with their own qualities and reasons for existence.

Medium Learn More

On top of where the music comes from, what is plays out of is just as (if not more) important to the quality of the audio.

Community

The audiophile community is vast, diverse, and growing. While it once was limited to those aiming to improve recorded audio, it now covers a large community more generally acceptable as "people who love sound."
